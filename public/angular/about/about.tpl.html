Listen Up Crusader!

My name is Jameson, and I am the founder of the Fasting Crusade, your community for Christian Fasting!

The idea for the Fasting Crusade came to me as I was doing a 24-hour fast with a fellow Christian. I realized that if I was going to fast with a companion, I wanted a way to interact with him. What I needed was a way to connect with him so we could inspire and lift each other up. I was given the idea for a fasting network almost instantly!

I couldnt stop there. I wanted more.

What we need is a spiritual revolution to praise something greater than ourselves. We want to invite the whole church to join us in this endeavor. We want to invite you to our Crusade!

We are here for you because we believe in the Christian Community!
